I am a fourth year Ph.D. candidate at UCLA HCI Research advised by Prof. Xiang 'Anthony' Chen. My research interest lies on the intersection at Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Robotics and Augmented Reality. More specifically, I develop tools to seamlessly integrate the digital and the physical world in the fields of Robotics and Augmented Reality.

In summer 2021, I did a research intern at Adobe Research working with Rubaiat Habib Kazi, Li-Yi Wei and Stephen DiVerdi. In summer 2019, I was a research intern at Palo Alto Research Center (PARC). I was a robotic engineer intern in summer 2018 at DMAI.

I received my B.E. in Naval Architecture and Marine Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2017.
